About Me


I am UX/UI Designer with a passion for crafting design solutions to solve real-world user problems - I am intrigued by the scope of design and how simple UI designs have a bigger impact on user experience. My design solutions are backed by user research, usability testing, and iterations - I am a firm believer of following a user-centered design process to develop solutions that help today's user accomplish their goals in the most efficient manner. Having worked in Human Resources for 5+ years has helped me understand the importance of empathizing with the user to discover the pain points and provide relevant solutions.
